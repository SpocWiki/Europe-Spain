# [[Pyrenees]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Pyrenees,12431]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Pyrenees** are a mountain range straddling the border of France and Spain. 
> They extend nearly 500 km (310 mi) from their union with the Cantabrian Mountains 
> to Cap de Creus on the Mediterranean coast, 
> reaching a maximum elevation of 3,404 metres (11,168 ft) at the peak of Aneto.
>
> For the most part, the main crest forms a political divide 
> between the states of Spain and France, 
> with the microstate of Andorra sandwiched in between. 
> 
> Historically, the Crown of Aragon and the Kingdom of Navarre 
> extended on both sides of the mountain range.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pyrenees)
