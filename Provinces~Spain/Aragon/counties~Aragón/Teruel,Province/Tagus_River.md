## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Tagus ( TAY-gəs; Spanish: Tajo [ˈtaxo] ; Portuguese: Tejo [ˈtɛʒu]) 
> is the longest river in the Iberian Peninsula. 
> 
> The river rises in the Montes Universales between Cuenca and Teruel, 
> in mid-eastern Spain, flows 1,007 km (626 mi), generally westward, 
> and empties into the Atlantic Ocean at Lisbon.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tagus)
